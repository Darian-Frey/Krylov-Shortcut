# Project CODE-GEO: Literature Map & Citations

The **Krylov-Shortcut** is a phenomenological extension of the following established research programs.

## 📡 Empirical Benchmarks (The "Hard Locks")
* **Sainadh, U. S., et al.** (2019). "Attosecond angular streaking and tunnelling time in atomic hydrogen." *Nature*, 568(7750), 75-77. 
    * *Role:* Provided the 1.8 as upper limit for Hydrogen tunneling used for the CODE-GEO atomic lock.
* **Keller, U.** (2020). "Attosecond angular streaking and tunneling time." *Journal of Modern Optics*.
    * *Role:* Context for multi-electron vs. single-electron timing discrepancies.

## 🧠 Theoretical Framework (Complexity & Tunneling)
* **Parker, D., et al.** (2019). "A Universal Operator Growth Hypothesis." *Physical Review X*, 9(041017).
    * *Role:* Foundation for Krylov complexity and the linear growth of Lanczos coefficients.
* **Balasubramanian, V., et al.** (2022). "Quantum chaos and the complexity of spread of states." *Physical Review D*, 106(046007).
    * *Role:* Derived the "Slope-Dip-Ramp-Plateau" structure of spread complexity used in our tunneling model.
* **Hauge, E. H., & Støvneng, J. A.** (1989). "Tunneling times: a critical review." *Reviews of Modern Physics*, 61(917).
    * *Role:* Standard reference for phase time and the Hartman Effect paradox.
* **Landauer, R., & Martin, T.** (1994). "Barrier interaction time in tunneling." *Reviews of Modern Physics*, 66(217).
    * *Role:* Grounding for the "reconstruction" interpretation vs. traditional traversal.

## 🌌 Holographic Origins
* **Susskind, L.** (2016). "Computational Complexity and Black Hole Horizons." *Fortschritte der Physik*.
* **Almheiri, A., Dong, X., & Harlow, D.** (2015). "Bulk Proposal from Quantum Error-Correcting Codes." *JHEP*.
